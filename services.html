OUR SERVICES

PHARMACY

The hospital has an in house pharmacy run by experienced pharmacists from where the patients are provided all the drugs and medicines. To save the patient unnecessary hassle of moving out of the hospital for the purchase of medicine, the Hospital Pharmacy operates round the clock.

RADIOLOGY

  • Digital X-Ray
  • Sonography
  • Portable X-Ray
  • Portable Sonography
  • Color Doppler

I.C.U

It is an ultra modern set-up with all the facilities and well equipped with the latest models of new generation monitoring devices.Advanced monitoring system connected to central monitor.

  • ECG monitor (life scope 6 and 9) with all the para-meters like ECG, Pulse, Resp. Capnography, Pulse Oximetry, CVP, NIBP, Cranial pressure. PCWP, Arterial pressure.
  • Bed side central oxygen, vacuum suction, compressed air supply ECG machines, defibrillators, Pulse-oximeters, nebulisers, infusion pumps and syringe pumps, Boyle’s machine.
  • Facility of CArm and ERCP.
  • One operation theatre with laminar air flow.

DIALYSIS UNIT

Hospital has two state of the art Dialysis machine to provide dialysis for the needy patient.

OPERATION THEATER

  • Hospital has four state of the art operation theatres.
  • Advanced Laparoscopy and Endoscopy facilities.
  • Facility of CArm and ERCP.
  • One operation theatre with laminar air flow.
